Directions

By road

Bradford is connected to the motorway network by the M62 and M606, which we recommend you use if arriving from the south, east or west.

Use the A629/A650 via Skipton if arriving from the north-west, or the A1 or A19 if arriving from the north-east.

Sat. Nav. postcode - use BD7 1AZ

By public transport

If you’re getting the bus to Bradford, or arriving by train at Forster Square or Bradford Interchange, the University is 15 minutes’ walk from the city centre.

Go through City Park and continue past the Alhambra Theatre up Great Horton Road. The campus is about 300 metres up this hill, just beyond the Bradford College.

Parking

The University of Bradford allows for free parking over weekends - feel free to park on campus if you're arriving by car.
