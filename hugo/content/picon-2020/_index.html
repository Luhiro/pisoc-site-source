What is PiCon?

The Pi Society team would like introduce PiCon - a student-led tech conference for like-minded students from around the UK. Great speakers, great community, a CTF competition, no entry fee and a free lunch - what more could you ask for?

When and where?

PiCon 2020 takes place on March 21st, 2020 in the Richmond building of the Univeristy of Bradford.
