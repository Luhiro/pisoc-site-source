What is PiCon?

The Pi Society team would like to introduce PiCon - a student-led tech conference for like-minded students from around the UK. Great speakers, great community, a CTF competition, no entry fee and a free lunch - what more could you ask for?

Many thanks to our sponsors and to everyone that has helped us organise PiCon:
BCS, IET, UBU and many others!

When and where?

PiCon 2020 takes place on March 21st, 2020 in the Richmond building of the University of Bradford.

Directions for finding your way onto the campus and into Richmond can be found here.
