Speakers

Christina Lovelock

Christina is an experienced IT leader and author.

Presenting...

Self-Awareness in System Development

A talk on self-awareness and its role in the life of a successful IT professional. With communication and collaboration being key to project delivery and organisational success, how we behave as IT professionals is more important than ever. This talk explores common behaviour traps that we can fall into and suggests tools that can facilitate self-reflection and improve self-awareness.

Andrew Shaw

I am a proactive, versatile and dedicated Software Testing Professional.

Presenting...

How to think like a tester

A talk and workshop on the causes of software failure & why software testing plays a vital role in the software development life cycle. The presentation includes fun and practical exercises on how to think like a testing professional, and how automated testing can add value to software testing.

Peter Taylor

Over 30 years experience in Information Systems and Security, Development, Project Management.

Presenting...

How to tell the Black from the White Hats

A talk on security testing, ethical hacking and social engineering from a business perspective. The talk provides an overview of the practice of "penetration testing" from the perspective of a long term consumer of pen-tests, and answers interesting questions related to security in the tech industry.
